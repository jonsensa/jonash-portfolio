## The method of creation has shifted.

I don't know what to feel. It is kind of annoying to see AI trying to complete my sentences, trying to guess what I am going to write next. This is me writing each word by myself going against the will of AI.

Not that this means anything, but are we actually heading to a future where micro-decisions are outsourced to AI. This is super weird to see everything get automated, scary and weird. 

All this while the future is uncertain, which always was, I can only remember the quote from Bhagavad Gita "Karmanye Vadhikaraste Ma Phaleshu Kadachana" : You have the right to and only to work but are not entitled to the fruits of your actions. Not that I believe in god religiously, I'm an agnostic myself, but somehow somewhere this quote stayed with me.

All I ever hope from the future is that people don't lose their originality, especially with creation of any kind.
